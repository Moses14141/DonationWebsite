About Wokovuway

Bringing Hope, Restoring Dignity, Changing Lives

Wokovuway is more than just an organization—it’s a movement committed to uplifting the most vulnerable in our communities. Founded with a passion to address the struggles of those affected by conflict and poverty, we operate in regions like the war-torn streets of Goma, DRC, and beyond, ensuring no one is left behind.

Through targeted initiatives, we deliver impactful support, including:

  • Health Support: Access to medical aid, mental health counseling, and emergency care for those in crisis.
  • Food Security: Nutritious meals and resources to combat hunger for families in need.
  • Shelters: Safe, temporary housing for individuals displaced by war or disaster.
  • Education: Learning opportunities for children impacted by conflict, paving the way for a brighter future.

Every life we touch represents a step toward a more equitable world. With the support of our partners and donors, we’re rebuilding hope—one person, one family, one community at a time.

Our Mission

We harness the power of community, compassion, and resilience to transform lives and break the cycles of conflict, poverty, and displacement.

Our mission is to empower vulnerable populations—particularly women and children—by providing access to quality education, comprehensive healthcare, and sustainable solutions that promote long-term development and self-reliance.

Our Vision

We strive to create a world where every individual can live with dignity and self-sufficiency, free from the burdens of poverty and injustice.

We envision thriving, resilient communities built on the foundations of education, economic empowerment, and sustainable growth, where every person has the chance to reach their full potential.

Our Values

  • Compassion: Acting with empathy, kindness, and respect toward all we serve.
  • Community: Building unity and fostering collaboration among individuals and partners.
  • Resilience: Inspiring perseverance and equipping people with tools for self-sufficiency.
  • Education: Unlocking opportunities through knowledge for future generations.
  • Sustainability: Implementing solutions designed for lasting, positive change.
  • Integrity: Upholding transparency, honesty, and accountability in all we do.
