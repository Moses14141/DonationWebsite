Volunteer with Wokovuway

Make a Difference

Join us in our mission to empower communities in the Democratic Republic of Congo and beyond. Volunteers are the heart of Wokovuway, bringing skills, passion, and dedication to our humanitarian efforts. Whether you’re local or international, your time and energy can create lasting impact. Last updated: March 13, 2025.

Why Volunteer with Us?

  • Impact Lives: Directly contribute to improving education, health, and livelihoods.
  • Gain Experience: Build skills in humanitarian work, teamwork, and cross-cultural collaboration.
  • Community Connection: Work alongside inspiring individuals and communities.
  • Flexibility: Opportunities range from short-term projects to ongoing roles, in-person or remote.

Current Opportunities

Community Educator

Location: Goma, DRC (In-Person)

Description: Teach basic literacy and skills to children and adults in displaced communities.

Time Commitment: 3-6 months, 10-15 hours/week

Fundraising Support

Location: Remote

Description: Assist with online campaigns, donor outreach, and event planning.

Time Commitment: Flexible, 5-10 hours/week

Logistics Coordinator

Location: Goma, DRC (In-Person)

Description: Help distribute aid supplies and manage local operations.

Time Commitment: 1-3 months, 20 hours/week

Opportunities are subject to change. Contact us for the latest openings.

How to Apply

Ready to get started? Follow these steps:

  1. Review Opportunities: Check the roles above or contact us for more options.
  2. Submit Interest: Fill out the form below or email us at volunteer@wokovuway.org with your name, skills, and availability.
  3. Interview: We’ll reach out to discuss your fit and next steps.
  4. Get Started: After onboarding, you’ll join our volunteer team!

By applying, you agree to our Privacy Policy and Terms of Use.
