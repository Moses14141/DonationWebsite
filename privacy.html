Privacy Policy

Introduction

Wokovuway Nonprofit Organization is dedicated to safeguarding the privacy of all individuals. This Privacy Policy outlines the methods of collecting, using, disclosing, and protecting personal information when interacting with the website, making donations, or engaging with the services offered. By using the services, individuals consent to the terms set forth in this policy. The policy was last updated on March 11, 2025.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and postal address when you donate, contact us, or fill out forms on our website.
  • Non-Personal Information: Browser type, IP address, and usage data collected via cookies and analytics tools to improve our services.
  • Donation Details: Information about your donations (e.g., type, additional details) to process and acknowledge your contributions.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process donations and provide receipts or acknowledgments.
  • Communicate with you about our programs, events, and updates (with your consent).
  • Improve our website and services through analytics and feedback.
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect our rights.

Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share it with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors (e.g., payment processors, email services) who assist us, bound by confidentiality agreements.
  • Partners: Organizations like Médecins Sans Frontières or MenEngage for joint initiatives, with your consent where required.
  • Legal Authorities: If required by law or to protect Wokovuway’s interests.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures (e.g., encryption, secure servers) to protect your information. However, no online transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information by contacting us at info@wokovuway.org.
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time via email preferences.
  • Request information about how your data is used.

Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience, analyze traffic, and personalize content. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted here with the updated date. Continued use of our services after changes implies acceptance.
