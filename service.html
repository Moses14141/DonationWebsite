Our Services

Empowering Communities

At Wokovuway Organization, we provide a range of humanitarian services aimed at uplifting vulnerable populations in the Democratic Republic of Congo and beyond. Our programs are designed to address immediate needs while fostering long-term resilience and growth. Last updated: March 13, 2025.

Humanitarian Aid

Emergency Relief

We deliver critical supplies such as food, water, shelter, and medical aid to communities affected by conflict, displacement, or natural disasters.

Health Services

Our mobile clinics and health outreach programs provide essential medical care, vaccinations, and maternal health support to underserved areas.

Education and Training

Literacy Programs

We offer basic education to children and adults, helping them gain literacy and numeracy skills to improve their opportunities.

Vocational Training

Through workshops and skill-building courses, we empower individuals with trades like tailoring, carpentry, and agriculture to achieve self-sufficiency.

Community Empowerment

Women’s Empowerment

We support women with resources, training, and microfinance opportunities to start small businesses and lead their communities.

Youth Development

Our programs engage young people through mentorship, leadership training, and recreational activities to build a brighter future.

Get Involved

Want to support our services? You can make a difference by:

  • Donating to fund our programs.
  • Volunteering your time and skills.
  • Contacting us to learn about partnership opportunities.
