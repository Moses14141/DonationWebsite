Become a volunteer

Become a valued member of our dedicated volunteer team and make a real difference in the lives of underprivileged children in Goma. Your time and skills are essential in supporting their personal growth. Join Wokovuway Humanitarian Organization and help us drive positive change.

Happy Giving

Experience the joy of giving by supporting Wokovuway Humanitarian Organization with any non-financial contribution you may have. Every contribution, no matter the size, brings happiness and hope to those in need.

Donation

Make a lasting impact through your financial support. Your donations enable us to continue our mission of improving the personal development of underprivileged children in Goma. Whether it's a one-time donation or a recurring contribution, together, we can create a brighter future for these children.

Invest in Tomorrow: Enabling Underprivileged Children through Education and Creativity

Informal Education

Empower young minds through our informal education initiatives. We believe in providing accessible and engaging learning experiences for underprivileged children in Congo. Explore how we foster a love for learning and equip these children with essential skills for a brighter future.

Creativity

Unlocking the creative potential of every child is at the heart of our mission. Discover the stories of resilience and innovation as we elevate talents and creativity among underprivileged youth in Congo. Join us in cultivating a culture of self-expression and imagination.

Charity

Compassion in action: our charity programs make a direct impact on the lives of those in need. Learn about the various ways we extend a helping hand to vulnerable communities in Congo. Your support enables us to provide essential resources and assistance to those who need it most.
