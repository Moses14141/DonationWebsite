About Wokovuway

Bringing Hope, Restoring Dignity, Changing Lives

Wokovuway is more than just an organization—it is a movement dedicated to uplifting the vulnerable in our communities. From the war-torn streets of Goma to struggling families in need, we believe that no one should be left behind.

Through our various initiatives, we provide:

  • Health Support: Medical aid, mental health resources, and emergency care.
  • Food Security: Nutritious meals for families facing hunger.
  • Shelters: Safe havens for those who have lost their homes.
  • Education: Opportunities for children impacted by war and crisis.

Every life we touch is a step toward a better world. Together, we can rebuild hope, one person at a time.

Our Mission

We believe in the power of community, compassion, and resilience to transform lives and break cycles of conflict, poverty, and displacement.

Our mission is to empower vulnerable populations—especially women and children—by providing access to education, healthcare, and sustainable solutions that foster long-term development.

Our Vision

To create a world where every individual has the opportunity to lead a dignified, self-sufficient life, free from poverty and injustice.

We envision resilient communities that thrive through education, economic empowerment, and sustainable development.

Our Values

  • Compassion: We believe in empathy, kindness, and respect for all individuals.
  • Community: Strengthening communities by fostering unity and collaboration.
  • Resilience: Encouraging self-sufficiency and perseverance in the face of challenges.
  • Education: Providing knowledge and skills to empower future generations.
  • Sustainability: Implementing long-term solutions that create lasting impact.
  • Integrity: Upholding transparency, honesty, and accountability in all our actions.
